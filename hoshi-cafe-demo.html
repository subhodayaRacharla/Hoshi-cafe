A star was born in
the heart of Madhapur

Hoshi — meaning "star" in Japanese — is more than a cafe. It's a space where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee meets the warmth of community. Our counter-centric layout puts the baristas at the heart of everything, creating an experience where coffee isn't just served — it's performed.

Whether you're here for a morning espresso, a late-night latte, or our signature chicken sandos — every visit is designed to feel like a constellation aligning just for you.
